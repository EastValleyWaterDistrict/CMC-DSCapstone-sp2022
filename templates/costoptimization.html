Cost Optimization Model

The deafult value for all boosters, wells, and PRVs is 100%. If a booster, well, or PRV cannot move water at its listed capacity (for example maintenance reasons), input the percentage capacity that the plant is capable of below. This will automatically adjust the model. If a plant has been taken offline, input 0%.
